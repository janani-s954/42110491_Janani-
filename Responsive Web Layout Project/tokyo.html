TOKYO

Tokyo is the capital and largest city of the Japan, known for its rich history, iconic landmarks, and cultural diversity. It is a global center for finance, education, and the arts, with attractions like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum.

Tokyo also boasts historic sites such as the Meiji Shrine and the Imperial Palace. A global center for fashion, entertainment, and cuisine, Tokyo offers a unique mix of old and new, making it one of the most dynamic cities in the world.
