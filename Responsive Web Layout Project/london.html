LONDON

London is the capital and largest city of the United Kingdom, known for its rich history, iconic landmarks, and cultural diversity. It is a global center for finance, education, and the arts, with attractions like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum.

The city has a vibrant mix of old and new, blending historic architecture with modern innovations. London is also famous for its diverse population and a wide range of cultural events and festivals throughout the year.
